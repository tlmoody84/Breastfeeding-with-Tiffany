About Us

Founded in 2010, Mother's Milk is a collection of International Board-Certified Lactation Consultants who conduct online, video appointments to help guide parents through the breastfeeding process. We’re here to offer you the best evidence-based advice, support, and guidance to help you reach your breastfeeding goals. We advocate for what you want and work closely with your primary care team to provide you with the best breastfeeding support you need.

Our commitment to Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

Here at Mother's Milk we recognize that families come in many forms. We are committed to creating a diverse and inclusive workforce to better support all parents regardless of race, color, sex, gender identity, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, age, marital status, or ability. Whatever your feeding goals are, we are here to help.

Founder Tiffany Sharpe

Mother of five breastfed children. Breastfeeding is definitely my passion. Bonding with my children was such a special time while nursing. I enjoyed every moment. Just a little bit of encoragement can go a long way.

I created my company once I gave birth to my 2nd child Niko, who was experiencing a lot of difficulty latching. He had silent reflux which made it a really challenging postpartum experience. I had very little guidance around lactation. I wasn’t getting any sleep. I felt exhausted and desperate having no knowledge of where to go for support.

This is why I decided to create a company where no woman has to struggle with the lack of knowledge from nursing.

My personal purpose is to support as many mothers and babies as we can around the world. My promise to you is that we will always be here, and we will do everything that we can to help support and care for you and your baby. Tiffany~
